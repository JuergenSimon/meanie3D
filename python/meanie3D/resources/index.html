How to read bars

The length of the bar is proportional to track length. The labels signify the length, followed by the number of tracks of that length in brackets. Click on a bar to restrict the graph to tracks of that length.
Try the following actions:
  • Drag nodes around
  • Double-click on a node to restrict the graph the node's track
  • Hover over a node to get more info

How to read nodes

Nodes are colored by track id. Older nodes are lighter in color, newer nodes are darker.

How to read lines

Gray lines are continuations
Blue lines are merges
Red lines are splits
